# About Sustainability Signals

Sustainability Signals is a platform designed to provide actionable insights and analytics on sustainability performance for companies, sectors, and markets. Our mission is to empower organizations, investors, and individuals with transparent, data-driven signals that support responsible decision-making and drive positive environmental, social, and governance (ESG) outcomes.

## Key Features
- **Real-time ESG Analytics:** Track and visualize sustainability metrics and trends across companies and sectors.
- **Interactive Dashboards:** Explore sector heatmaps, momentum charts, and KPI cards for a comprehensive view of sustainability performance.
- **Data-Driven Insights:** Leverage advanced data sources and methodologies to uncover meaningful sustainability signals.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Intuitive design for seamless navigation and actionable reporting.

## Our Vision
We believe in a future where sustainability is at the core of every decision. By making ESG data accessible and understandable, we aim to accelerate the transition to a more sustainable and equitable world.
